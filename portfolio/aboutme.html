A Little Bit About Me!

I’m currently a graduate student at University of Michigan - Ann Arbor studying information science and human-computer interaction. I’m originally from San Francisco and I received my undergraduate degree in psychology, philosophy and visual arts at Boston University.

As a product designer, I take into account the business’s needs, the back-end’s needs, and the user’s needs when approaching a problem. As someone who comes from a background in psychology, I always try to understand human behaviors and what motivates a user’s decision making process. I value using different user research methods, such as A/B testings and scales to weigh different metrics, such as usability and interaction style.
